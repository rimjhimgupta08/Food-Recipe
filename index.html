BEE HUAT FOOD PTE LTD

Bee Huat Food Industries has dedicated ourself to the manufacturing and distribution of noodles. With over 70 years of experience, we are renowned for our commitment to customers, serving resellers, wet markets, and hawkers in Singapore. We offers a wide range of products, including plain noodles, wanton noodles, wanton skins, fresh noodles, kway teow, rice vermicelli, and more.
